Lynsay's Daily Dog Walks

                

Hi, I'm Lynsay and welcome to Lynsay's daily dog walks page. I am a dog walker in Middleton, Leeds and excited to connect with owners and their much-loved pups.

All my life I have loved walking dogs, from taking my Labrador, Jamie out before school to now walking by beautiful German Shorthaired Pointer, Bolton.

My family has owned a dog walking business in Scotland since 2008 which I have managed during vacations and busy periods. Through this work I have over 5 years’ experience with a wide range of dog breeds of all ages. I am reliable, trustworthy and will ensure your dog is in a safe and fun environment.

Please contact me on 07901 380074 to discuss your dog’s individual needs.

Walks start from £9.50.

Discounts available for OAPs, registered disabled, OADs (Old Aged Dogs) and dogs from same household.
